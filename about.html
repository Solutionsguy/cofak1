about Chrity


Welcome to COFAK, a vibrant charity dedicated to unlocking potential and fostering sustainable growth within our communities. Our core mission is to create meaningful employment opportunities for young individuals, empowering them to contribute positively to society and thrive in today's dynamic world.
The heart of our efforts is a deep commitment to engaging with diverse groups, sharing knowledge, and exploring innovative income-generating avenues. Through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects, we strive to equip the youth with the skills, confidence, and resources they need to succeed.
Our journey is one of collaboration and shared vision. We work closely with local businesses, educational institutions, and community leaders to nurture an ecosystem that supports and celebrates the aspirations of young people.

In pursuit of expanding our impact and reaching more lives, we are actively seeking partnerships and sponsorships. These collaborations are vital in scaling our initiatives and ensuring the sustainability of our programs.

Leading our passionate team is Mr. Ben, our esteemed director. With a wealth of experience and a heart full of dedication, Mr. Ben guides us in our mission, inspiring both our team and the communities we serve.

Join us in making a difference. Together, we can pave the way for a brighter, more inclusive future.
